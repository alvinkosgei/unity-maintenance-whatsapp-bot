## How to Use the Chatbot

### Step 1: Initial Contact

1. Open WhatsApp and initiate a chat with the chatbot
2. Send a greeting message (e.g., "Hello")
3. The chatbot will respond with a welcome message and three quick-reply options

### Step 2: Choose Your Action

The chatbot presents three main options:

- **Report an Issue** - Report problems related to amenities maintenance, apartment maintenance, billing, etc.
- **Make an Enquiry** - Raise questions about processes, policies, timings, etc.
- **Any Suggestions** - Share feedback or ideas unrelated to a specific issue

## Feature Details

### 1. Reporting an Issue

#### Step 1: Initiate Report
- Tap the **Report an Issue** button
- The bot sends a template message with a "Report an issue" button
- Tap to open the two-part form

#### Step 2: Fill Out the Form

**Screen I - Contact & Location:**
| Field | Instructions |
|-------|--------------|
| Name | Enter your full name |
| Contact Number | Confirm or edit the mobile number WhatsApp auto-fills |
| Estate | Select from: Unity East, Unity West, Unity One, Unity Gardens, Unity Silver, or Unity Parkside |
| Address | Fill in Block/Phase, Apartment No., etc. (appears after selecting estate) |

Press **Continue** to proceed to the next screen.

**Screen II - Issue Details:**
| Field | Instructions |
|-------|--------------|
| Category | Choose one: Amenities Maintenance, Apartment Maintenance, Billing, Common Area Maintenance, Community Matters, or Customer Service |
| Others | For issues not covered by categories above |
| Propose Date and Time | Select a date; technicians work Mon–Fri 08:00–17:00 and Sat 08:00–12:00 |

#### Step 3: Submit

Once all required fields are complete, the **Complete** button turns green. Tap it to submit your request.

#### Step 4: Receive Ticket

- **Immediate:** You'll receive a confirmation message ("We've received your concern...")
- **Shortly After:** A separate notification with assigned technician's name, phone number, and scheduled arrival time and date

#### Step 5: Confirm Technician Arrival

Once the technician arrives, you'll receive a message asking for confirmation:

> "Please confirm if the technician has arrived at your location to fix the issue (Ticket#: XXXX)."

Quick-reply options:
- **Technician Arrived**
- **Technician Not Arrived**

#### Step 6: Confirm Issue Resolution

After work is completed, confirm resolution status:

> "Kindly confirm if the technician has resolved your issue."

Options:
- **Issue Resolved**
- **Issue Not Resolved**

#### Step 7: Submit Feedback

Once you confirm the issue is resolved:

1. The bot sends a template message with a "Submit Feedback" button
2. Tap the button to open a short rating/comment form
3. Submit feedback to complete the cycle
4. Receive a thank you message confirming successful submission

---

### 2. Making an Enquiry

1. Tap the **Make an Enquiry** quick-reply option
2. The bot sends a template message with a "Submit Enquiry" button
3. Tap the button to open the enquiry form
4. Fill in your question/enquiry and tap **Submit**
5. Receive a confirmation message

---

### 3. Sending a Suggestion

1. Tap the **Any Suggestion** quick-reply option
2. The bot sends a template message with a "Submit Suggestion" button
3. Tap the button to open the suggestion form
4. Enter your idea or feedback and tap **Submit**
5. Receive a confirmation message that your suggestion has been logged
